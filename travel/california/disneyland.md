# Disneyland Plan for Two Adults

## Trip Details

*   **Duration:** 2 days (Day 2 is a half day)
*   **Ages:** 35-year-old adults
*   **Interests:** Thrill rides, immersive experiences, nighttime shows, things at night.

## General Tips

*   **Park Hopper Tickets:** Get Park Hopper tickets to be able to visit both parks on the same day.
*   **Genie+ and Lightning Lane:** Consider purchasing to save time waiting in lines for popular rides.
*   **Single Rider Lines:** Utilize these for shorter wait times if you don't mind riding separately.
*   **Disneyland App:** Download for wait times, park maps, show schedules, and mobile ordering.
*   **Arrive Early:** Get to the park at least 30 minutes before opening to maximize your time.

## Nighttime Activities

### Shows

*   **Fantasmic!:** A show with floats, characters, and special effects on the Rivers of America in Disneyland.
*   **World of Color:** A water and light show in Disney California Adventure.
*   **Fireworks:** Held at Disneyland, with projections on Sleeping Beauty Castle and Main Street, U.S.A.

### Disneyland After Dark Events

*   Separately ticketed events with themes like Star Wars Nite or Sweethearts' Nite.
*   Offer shorter wait times, unique entertainment, and themed food.
*   Allow adults to wear costumes.

### Dining and Lounges

*   **Oga's Cantina:** An immersive Star Wars-themed bar in Galaxy's Edge.
*   **Trader Sam's Enchanted Tiki Bar:** A popular tiki bar at the Disneyland Hotel.
*   **Lamplight Lounge:** A Pixar-themed lounge at Disney California Adventure.

## Classic & Nostalgic Rides

*   **Peter Pan's Flight:** A classic dark ride that takes you soaring over London and Neverland.
*   **Disneyland Railroad:** A relaxing train ride that circles the park.
*   **Jungle Cruise:** A humorous boat ride through a simulated jungle.
*   **King Arthur Carrousel:** A classic carousel in Fantasyland.
*   **Mark Twain Riverboat:** A leisurely boat ride around Tom Sawyer Island.
*   **Mr. Toad's Wild Ride:** A whimsical dark ride in Fantasyland.
*   **Snow White's Enchanted Wish:** A classic Fantasyland dark ride.

## Dining

### Themed Restaurants

*   **Blue Bayou Restaurant:** Located inside the Pirates of the Caribbean ride, offering a unique, dimly lit dining experience. (Disneyland Park)
*   **Carthay Circle Restaurant:** An elegant restaurant with a 1930s Hollywood theme. (Disney California Adventure Park)
*   **Wine Country Trattoria:** A restaurant with a Tuscan villa theme and a large wine list. (Disney California Adventure Park)
*   **Trader Sam's Enchanted Tiki Bar:** A popular, interactive tiki bar. (Disneyland Hotel)
*   **Napa Rose:** An upscale restaurant with a focus on California cuisine and wine. (Disney's Grand Californian Hotel & Spa)
*   **Lamplight Lounge:** A Pixar-themed gastropub with waterfront views. (Disney California Adventure Park)
*   **Flo's V8 Cafe:** A 1950s-style diner in Cars Land. (Disney California Adventure Park)
*   **Rancho del Zocalo:** A Mexican restaurant with a vibrant, colorful setting. (Disneyland Park)
